Virtual Paper Label Printer
The printer receives an A4 or A5 page, while only the selected label strip is used. Place the strip on the left, right, top or bottom edge; the fixed 20 mm pickup gap follows the feed direction.
Virtual label margins (mm)
These margins sit inside the chosen label dimensions. Existing TO/FROM margins remain part of the label design.
Use the single Preview window to inspect label coordinates, drag QR/image items, or switch to the clean Final view.
Envelope Printer
Enter the real envelope or label dimensions, then drag each item into position. TO starts in the centre and FROM at the top left. Mouse and touch are supported.
Envelope margins (mm)
All draggable items remain inside this safe printable area.
Open the single Preview window. Inspect mode shows coordinates and draggable item dimensions; Final mode removes all guides. QR codes and images appear when their corresponding data is available.
